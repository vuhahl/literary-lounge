About Me!

Hi, I'm Mariana!

I'm a book lover who loves to read and share my thoughts and opinions on the latest books I've read. I originally made this blog for my college class but then it became something more.

On this little website of mine, you'll find my book favorites/ratings and hopefully in the future you can find author interviews and discussions on various book-related topics. I enjoy reading a wide range of genres, from thrillers and mysteries to romance and historical fiction, so I'm sure you'll find a book that interests you.

When I'm not reading or blogging, you can find me exploring new coffee shops, spending time with my family , or trying watching kdramas!
